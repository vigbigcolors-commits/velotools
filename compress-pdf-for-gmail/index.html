Compresses PDF (mixed stream codecs (Flate, DCT/JPEG, JPX, CCITT)) via PDF.js render at 96 DPI, JPEG 65%, pdf-lib reassembly — locked to Gmail.

Gmail: max 25 MB (26,214,400 bytes). Consumer Gmail attachment limit per message (Google Workspace may differ by admin policy). Preset web is locked on this URL.

Frequently asked questions

Why is the preset locked?
Page intent is Gmail (25 MB). Widget state must match URL or Google classifies the page as thin doorway content.
What if the PDF is still too large?
Still over 25 MB — try Screen preset or split the PDF first. Split at /split-pdf/ if the platform allows multiple files.
Is the file uploaded?
No. Client-side PDF.js + pdf-lib only.
Scanned vs text PDFs?
Scanned pages compress more. Text-heavy PDFs may stay above the cap — lower DPI preset or split.
