Compress JPG, PNG, WebP, and AVIF files up to 90% smaller while preserving visual quality. Convert to WebP (25–40% smaller than JPEG) or AVIF (up to 50% smaller) for modern browser performance. Beyond compression: batch process 15 images simultaneously, resize to exact dimensions with aspect ratio lock, crop with a rule-of-thirds guide, rotate and flip, apply 8 professional effect presets, adjust brightness/contrast/saturation, add Gaussian blur or pixelation for censoring sensitive information.
