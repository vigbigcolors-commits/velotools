Why VeloTools

The only tools that work for you — not against you

Most online tools have a dirty secret: they upload your files to remote servers, process them there, and keep copies longer than you'd expect. Your photos, your invoices, your documents — stored on servers you don't control.

VeloTools works completely differently. Every single operation runs inside your browser using modern Web APIs — the Canvas API, WebAssembly, and the File API. The most complete free image tool on the web

Most "free" image compressors offer one thing: a quality slider and a download button. VeloTools Image Compressor is built for professionals who need a full editing suite — without the subscription.

Compress JPG, PNG, WebP, and AVIF files up to 90% smaller while preserving visual quality that your audience won't notice. Convert between all major formats including the modern WebP and AVIF formats that cut file sizes 25–50% compared to JPEG with identical quality scores. Faster websites, better Core Web Vitals, happier visitors.

Beyond compression: resize to exact pixel dimensions with aspect ratio lock, rotate and flip for perfect orientation, apply 8 professional effect presets (grayscale, sepia, vivid, matte, and more), adjust brightness, contrast, saturation, and hue with live preview, add Gaussian blur or pixelation for censoring, and crop with precision. Converting to WebP for faster websites
Drop your JPEG or PNG, switch format to WebP, set quality to 80% — you'll get files 30–40% smaller with virtually identical visual quality. Modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) all support WebP natively. Use it for every image on your website.
💡 Pro tip: 80% quality WebP = better than 95% JPEG, at half the file size
02
Batch compressing product photos
Switch to Batch mode, drop up to 15 product images, set your target format and quality once, hit Process. All images compress in parallel. Download as a single ZIP. An entire e-commerce catalog in under a minute — no Photoshop, no plugins, no subscription.
💡 Pro tip: For e-commerce, target 100–200KB per product image for fast page loads
03
Resizing for social media
Every platform has different requirements — Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook all want different dimensions. Use the Resize panel with aspect ratio lock, enter your target dimensions, and export. The live preview shows exactly what will be cropped or scaled before you commit.
💡 Pro tip: Instagram square = 1080×1080px, LinkedIn cover = 1584×396px
04
Blurring sensitive content
The Blur panel offers Gaussian blur, pixelation, and sharpen modes. Use the Crop tool first to isolate the sensitive area, then apply blur. Perfect for screenshots with personal information, addresses, or names that need to be shared publicly without exposing private data.
💡 Pro tip: Pixelate at level 8+ to make text completely unreadable
