Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

🔒

The short version

Your files never leave your browser. We don't upload, store, or analyze your data. VeloTools processes everything locally on your device.

File processing

All file processing on VeloTools happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript and the Canvas API. When you compress an image, generate an invoice, or create a QR code, the files and data you enter are processed locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Analytics

We use basic anonymous analytics to understand which tools are most popular. This data is aggregated and never tied to individual users. We do not use cookies for tracking purposes.

Advertising

VeloTools displays advertisements through Google AdSense to support the free service. Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising at google.com/settings/ads.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at privacy@velotools.app
